Places I've Lived

Scotland (2023-Present)

I moved here to study. So far my experience has been pleasant, and I enjoy living here. I have bought a flat here, so it might be a place I stay for a long time, but we never know as I don't like staying in the same place for too long if it were my choice. In Scotland, I have visited many attractions and events, as well as different cafes and restaurants.

England (2014-2023)

I have lived here for a very long time, almost as much as I have lived in Latvia. When I came here I knew no English, so I had to start learning from scratch. I finished secondary school and A-Levels here. Life was fun but different since we lived in a city. We visited attractions, fun rooms, and everything felt new to me.

Latvia (2013-2014 and 2004-2012)

I have lived there since I was born. I went to school there, and life was simple. I used to go out with my friends every day to the park, go fishing, and swim in the lake whenever it was warm. It was a peaceful and joyful time in my life.

Cyprus (2012-2013)

We lived there only for a year, but during that time, I really enjoyed my life. Every weekend, we would go to new places for mountain biking and other events and sports. It was a fun and adventurous time for me and my family.
